This is my main project. It is the one I dedicate most of my time to when I have the spare time. It has seen quite a few overhauls and rewrites over time, as I become more experienced. What I love most about this project is just how much it has taught me and helped me grow. It it constantly requiring me to improve my skills in order to tackle any giving task I am working on and I wouldn't trade it for the world.

Many, many days ago, when I was first exploring the endless pit of programming I became enthralled to the wonders of computer graphics. It started out with me exploring game engines already out on shelves such as, Unreal and Unity. I had a lot of fun just messing around and learning about each of them but, I always found myself wondering how it all came together as a complete package. How does the scripting work in Unity? How does Unreal render its scenes? It was the root of those types of questions when I decided to branch out and begin to research such things. I wanted to learn, but more importantly, I wanted to get hands on experience with developing such a software.

It was an ambitious undertaking. Still is. But, looking back it has helped me develop my skills so much. It opened the doors to studying programming patterns, programming languages, flexible architecture designs, and a general sense of style. No matter how ambitious the task is - for real, an entire game engine, plus editor - no matter how many mistakes I make, I will keep going forward and chipping away at it, because at the end of the day I just enjoy it too much.

So, don't be surprised if you don't see a update for a while upon this project. It takes me time. I am only a single person and I have to take care of real life responsibilities too. At this point though I am beginning to find a nice ebb and flow to my work flow and going forward from here we should start seeing some more interesting features from this project.

With all that said, I hope you enjoy. Take care and happy coding.
